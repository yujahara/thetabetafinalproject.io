About the Project

Our potential chapter designed and built an outdoor communal space to support student wellness and collaboration. The project utilized the combination of research, engineering, fundraising, and construction to deliver a practical, durable, eco-friendly solution.

  • Goal #1: Provide students with a space for outdoor study and relaxation, promoting mental wellness and a healthy balance between academic and social life.
  • Goal #2: To create a new communal space in a community that encourages people to gather, connect, and collaborate, thereby strengthening the overall community.
  • Goal #3: To showcase the professionalism and technical skills of our class by successfully managing a complete engineering project from start to finish.

Showcase

Exploring the key features that make the Plug to Unplug Bench a self-sustaining and user-friendly addition to our community.

Sustainability

Our project is self sustainable thanks to us attaching a solar panel on the roof and using that to charge the battery, to power our inverter, so that anyone can charge their devices at any time.

Convinience

This project provides convinience to whomever is sitting at the bench because they do not have to go inside or leave nature to charge their electronics.
